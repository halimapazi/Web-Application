Introduction to Cyberthreats

Cyberthreats are a growing concern in today's digital age, and can take many forms. These threats can compromise sensitive information, disrupt critical infrastructure, and cause significant financial losses.

Common Types of Cyberattacks

  • Phishing attacks
  • Ransomware attacks
  • Malware attacks
  • Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) attacks
  • Man-in-the-middle (MitM) attacks

Tips for Protecting Against Cyberthreats

  1. Use strong and unique passwords
  2. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
  3. Keep your software and operating system up-to-date
  4. Be wary of suspicious emails and messages
  5. Use a reputable antivirus software
